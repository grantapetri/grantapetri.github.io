Engineering Tradeoffs

  • Client-side responsiveness is preserved for feel, while final outcomes remain server-owned.
  • Config-driven flexibility increases tuning speed but requires disciplined schema ownership.
  • AI group behavior improves combat dynamics but increases runtime vector computation cost.

Lessons Learned

  • Extraction shooters demand strong state integrity before feature scale.
  • Reusable frameworks reduce long-term content cost in multiplayer projects.
  • Authority boundaries must be defined early, not patched in later.

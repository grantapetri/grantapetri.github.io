Project

Slime Dungeon

Game jam project — 1 week build

Overview

Slime Dungeon is a game jam project completed in 1 week. The goal was to keep the design tight, iterate quickly, and ship a complete gameplay loop inside a strict deadline.

Focus Areas

  • Fast prototyping and iteration under time pressure
  • Clear combat and movement feel with minimal complexity
  • Scope control to ensure a playable, finished build
